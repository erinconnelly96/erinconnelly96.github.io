I am a PhD in the math department of University of Washington, with advisors Rekha Thomas and Sameer Agarwal. I have completed my thesis and am on track to receive my PhD. My reserach interests include algebraic geometry, computer vision, and geometric invariant theory. I am currently searching for a post-doc position.
